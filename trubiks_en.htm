Rubik's Snake  - Info


"RUBIK's Snake" is an ultimate toy - nobody wants to put it aside. Once started you can generate a tremendous amount of different figures and shapes. 

If you don't know Rubik's Snake have a look at the official site from Rubik's (now named Rubik's Twist).  

To "save the solutions" we had create a notation (language of description). This notation describes how to turn and twist the snake to get the different figures.

One of the most beautiful figures (easy figures, tough figures) from us you will see on the next sites. In addition all figures from manual are included.

We are interested in getting figures from other people via E-Mail. These shapes are shown on categories Fans & Friends and Victor's World. Our target is to construct a collection of the different figures.

Anybody who wants, can contact us and send other figures. We recommend our notation, but other descriptions are desired as well. We would include the new figure(s) in the collection, along with the name of sender.
